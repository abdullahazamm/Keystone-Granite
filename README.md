# Keystone Granite

**Project Link**: [https://keystonegraniteoh.com/](https://keystonegraniteoh.com/)

## Project Overview
For this project, I designed and customized a **WordPress website** for **Keystone Granite**, a flooring business based in the United States. The website was built using **Elementor** to create a flexible and visually appealing layout that aligns with the business’s brand. The site was optimized for both user experience and SEO to ensure maximum visibility.

## Key Features & Customization

### **1. Custom WordPress Design**
- Designed the website from scratch using **Elementor Page Builder**, ensuring that it was responsive and user-friendly. The custom design focused on showcasing the business's products, including various flooring materials, while providing an intuitive navigation experience.
- **WooCommerce Integration**: Implemented **WooCommerce** to facilitate e-commerce functionality, enabling the business to sell flooring products directly from the website.

### **2. CRM Integration**
- Integrated **Go High Level CRM** for effective lead tracking. This integration helps the business efficiently capture and manage leads, improving follow-up times and conversion rates.

### **3. SEO Optimization**
- Worked closely with the **SEO team** to enhance the website’s search engine visibility. I focused on optimizing the website structure, metadata, and on-page content to help the business rank higher in search engine results. Using **RankMath SEO**, I ensured the website adhered to best SEO practices, improving its chances of ranking in top positions.

### **4. Behavioral Analysis and Reporting**
- Integrated **Google Analytics** for detailed reporting on website traffic and user behavior, enabling the business to track performance and make data-driven decisions to further optimize the site.

## Development Process

### **1. Planning & Requirements**
The project began with gathering the client’s requirements and understanding their business needs. The design was aligned with the client’s vision, with a focus on creating an aesthetically pleasing site that is also functional for users looking to purchase flooring products.

### **2. Design & Customization**
Using **Elementor**, I created a custom design that matched the business’s branding. The site’s layout was designed to highlight the various flooring options and categories, with intuitive navigation and easy-to-find product details.

### **3. E-commerce Integration**
I integrated **WooCommerce** to allow the client to sell flooring products directly through the website. This included setting up product categories, checkout pages, and payment gateway integrations.

### **4. CRM Integration**
I implemented **Go High Level CRM** to streamline lead generation and follow-up. This integration helps the business track and manage customer inquiries, enabling more effective communication and conversion strategies.

### **5. SEO Optimization**
Working with the **SEO team**, I focused on optimizing the website for better search engine rankings. This included optimizing product pages, improving site structure, and setting up metadata using **RankMath SEO**. The site was optimized to enhance visibility on search engines, particularly for local searches.

### **6. Behavioral Analysis**
I integrated **Google Analytics** to provide in-depth reports on user behavior, allowing the client to track the most visited pages, popular products, and overall website performance. This data helps the business make informed decisions about future website improvements.

## Additional Information
This project helped **Keystone Granite** establish a professional online presence for their flooring business. By integrating **WooCommerce**, **Go High Level CRM**, and **RankMath SEO**, the website is now optimized for sales, lead generation, and visibility in search engines. The collaboration with the **SEO team** ensured that the website was positioned to rank in top search results, driving organic traffic to the site.
